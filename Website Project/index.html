Welcome to our Computer Science (CS) Classroom!

My name is Mr. Ralph S Scimeca, a Computer Science Educator

I became a teacher to empower students to become creators.

My individual teaching style focuses on:

  1. Project based learning,
    • Research shows our learning and application of concepts strengthens as we construct real-world solutions
  2. Problem-solving,
    • Computer Science at its core is focused on solving problems. As computer scientists we utilize Computational Thinking to help develop a framework of how to solve problems
  3. Self-expression,
    • As technology integrates into all our lives, we have a responsibility to develop physical or virtual environments for expression and inclusion. Technology should extend our capabilities and therefore understanding how we can better use our computers to become better versions of ourselves.
  4. and reflection.
    • Our access to a wide variety of resources can become crippling as resources compete for our attention. Effective computer scientists reflect on their role, management of time and overall goal.

My firm commitment revolves around equipping students with essential tools and knowledge. i strive to establish an inclusive, dynamic learning environment that sparks the growth of a new generation of compassionate innovators in the exciting realm of computer science.
